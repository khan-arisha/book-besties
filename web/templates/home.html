Why NYT Bestsellers?

We have a passion for books. We hope this website will allow book enthusiasts to find information about NYT best-selling books and authors from over the past century, and perhaps provide inspiration for their next literary experience.

Fun facts about NYT bestselling books

  • The NYT started compiling this list in 1931.
  • Danielle Steele has 116 books on the NYT bestsellers list.
  • "Oh, The Places You'll Go!" by Dr. Seuss is the longest running book on the NYT bestsellers list.
